About

ACADEMIA BJJ LIFESTYLE Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is a gym academy that will not only focus on teaching jiu-jitsu to its students. It offers the benefits of sport LIFESTYLE by providing through Gentle Art the skills needed to achieve your personal goals. Whenever a competitor or sports enthusiast, Academia BJJ Lifestyle will bring out the best in you in a friendly environment.

GOOD VIBES ONLY!

Our gym

Our gym was created by Professor Lucio Sergio dos Santos Third Degree Black Belt. From Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. Started learning Jiu-jitsu in 2000 with Professor Joelson Souza. Being highly competitive, he started competing after three months of training. His willingness to achieve results led him to dedicate himself to the art as a full-time job. His dedication got results. In December 2006, Lucio received my black Belt from Professor Joelson Sousa. In 2008, Lucio moved to the UK where he continued competing and opened his academy.

Here are some of Lucio's achievements during his competitor journey:

  • IBJJF World Nogi Champion 2019
  • 2x IBJJF Pan American Champion 2019/2022
  • 2x IBJJF European Champion 2013/2020
  • IBJJF Brazil National NoGi Champion 2012
  • 2x IBJJF European NOGI Champion 2015/2023
  • 2x IBJJF European NOGI Champion 2015/2023
  • 4x Abu Dhabi Professional World Champion - 2014/2016/2017/2021
  • 4x Abu Dhabi Professional World Champion - 2014/2016/2017/2021
  • 2x IBJJF European International Master Champion 2019/2022
  • 2x IBJJF European International Master Champion 2019/2022
  • IBJJF Opens Multiple Times Champion Gi and Nogi Champion
  • 3X IBJJF British Open Champion
  • Abu Dhabi Grand Slam Tournament Champion
  • 2x IBJJF World Master Silver Medalist 2019/2022

With several years of competing. Lucio uses his experience to share knowledge with his students. The academy has currently black belts promoted and numerous athletes getting results on IBJJF, AJP, and UAEBJJF. "No result cannot be achieved without a process. All we have to do is trust and follow"

"Don't rush the process, Trust the process"

Lucio Sergio dos Santos
